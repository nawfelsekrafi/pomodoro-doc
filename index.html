what ?
- This is a productivity, fully responsive application built with Angular 12 and Firebase.
- Users can create accounts with -Email, Google - then Sign In to add projects, modify them, delete them and count time passed in working on them..
- The app contains a pomodoro (25mins) Timer, the ability to select a project to save time within it and when time's up the session will be saved.
- The app Also contains a dashboard page to show all your working timelines and your data.
- User can change the sound when time's up and also they can delete their account.
